Kids No-gi Classes

What are Kids No-gi Classes?

Build confidence, discipline, and self-defense skills.

Our Kids No-gi classes provide a fun and engaging environment where children can learn essential grappling techniques. These classes are designed to help kids of all skill levels improve their physical fitness, develop discipline, and gain confidence.

Led by experienced coaches, children will practice fundamental grappling moves and learn how to stay safe in different situations. Through teamwork and respect, they will grow both mentally and physically while having fun in a supportive setting.

Our Coach

Jed Hue

Jed Hue is an expert in No-gi grappling and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, with years of experience both training and competing at the highest levels. Known for his technical skills and dedication, Jed is passionate about teaching and helping students achieve their full potential on and off the mats.
